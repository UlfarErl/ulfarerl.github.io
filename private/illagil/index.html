Information about Illagil 15, 13, and 9

FOR SALE: Freehold-ownership land plots for residential construction available by the Þingvellir National Park UNESCO World Heritage Site in Iceland.

The plots are at 64°09'03.6"N 21°15'03.6"W, about 30km (15 miles) from Reykjavík, just outside the no-construction area of the National Park, and have easy access to Lake Þingellir and come with permits for boating on the lake.

The main portion of the land consists of plots Illagil 15 and Illagil 13, which together comprise a contiguous 13000 sq. meters (140 thousand sq. feet, or 3.2 acres) at the highest point of the land, with stunning views. In addition, a separate plot of land can be included: Illagil 9 is 6600 sq. meters (71 thousand sq. feet, or 1.6 acres) which lies at a lower, more easily accessible part of the land and is well suited for a guesthouse or a boathouse.



The land already has construction permits for a residential house at the highest point of Illagil 15, and a foundation for an architect-designed house has already been dug at the site, and an access road has been constructed along with providing electricity and water.
